Learning Centre

What we can achieve here can potentially alter the world: an educational model that works, a university devoid of ageism and a cattle market mentality - no entrance qualifications or graduation certificates but with so much more….

More and more people are reaching the conclusion that our education systems need to be developed towards more divergent thinking and the creation of an abundance of resources for all.

MaidSafe Foundation will establish a free learning facility where anyone can learn, share and develop what they are genuinely interested in at any stage in life, without silos or any obligations. This will help to advance creative solutions enormously to make the world a better place.
