Catrine Primary 1 Chinese Screens

Chinese dragon screens --- As part of their Chinese New Year topic, Primary 1 drew pictures of a dragon. These were then taken away and used a laser cutter to engrave the drawing and cut the backgrounds out on card. the children were shown videos of the laser cutter in action and then we used tissue paper to create our own dragon sun catchers.
